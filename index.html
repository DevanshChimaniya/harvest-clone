AMONG AGRICULTURAL COMPANIES

We are a prominent leader in the agribusiness, specializing in the cultivation, production and trading of animal feed and essential human food commodities such as corn, fruits, flour and vegetables. Since its inception, we have witnessed unmatched growth fuelled by an active foreign investment strategy, establishing various acquisitions and joint ventures with specialized feed and food producers worldwide. We are own and operate a large asset base including a land bank of 340 thousand acres, 4 forage pressing and production plants and 2 flour milling plants.
